About Us

Welcome to the lovely lap of nature to nurture your soul, mind and body. Devote a little of your quality time to your own welbeing before it is too late. We work with the objective to assist everyone to regain total health and allowing life energy to flow with its fullest potential.

Here at our centre you can also have the divine grace of Gurumatha Amma, who has been a real transformer for thousands of people all across the globe. She has established Vyasa Veda Peeta-A centre for self realisation at Sridhara Sri Gudda.

Sridhara Sri Gudda is a place to experience the universal Positivity and serenity. Along with treatment you can witness Nature's enormous beauty around you in various forms.

AIM

We concentrate on PANCHA-KOSHA's (five Psychic Sheaths), Thus treat the illness and promote health likewisely.
Annamaya Kosha
(Food-apparent sheath)

Composed of our physical body

Pranamaya Kosha
(Energy sheath)

Composed of vital force "Prana"

Manomaya Kosha
(Mind sheath)

Composed of our mind

Vijnanamaya Kosha
(Wisdom sheath)

Composed of wisdom or intellect

Anandamaya Kosha
(Bliss sheath)

Composed of supreme bliss, regarded as innermost sheath
