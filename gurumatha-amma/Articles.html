Jeevana Dharma Yoga




Jeevana Dharma Yoga is where Visnhu shakthi is dominant.

An individual acquires self knowledge as a result of penetrating insight increasing inner vision and improved effiency in work.

Jeevana Dharma Yoga is a life changing experience under the subtle guidence of Gurumatha Amma. During their association with Amma. During their association with Amma, aspirants devote their time to learn the "spiritual alphabet".

This is living life itself; the bliss is the consequence of your being.
