DASHADINOTSAVA

Dashadinotsava from 12.2018 to 01.01.2019 will highlight on various TECHNIQUES in MEDITATION. Her Devine Grace Gurumatha Amma has graciously agreed to conduct and guide the meditation sessions every evening during Dashadinotsava.

This meditation session is not only a boon to spiritual aspirants but going in the right path, right direction is even more important. We the children of Gurumatha Amma are the chosen ones to be in the company of living Guru. Apart form teachers training, Dashadinotsava also has a total spiritual health retreat for all the freshers. An intoduction to Meditation, Pranayama, Yoga and Naturopathy, Healthy food, Oil massage, Water therapies are part of this total health.

Dashadinotsava concludes on 1.1.2019. poojya Gurumatha Amma's 64th birthday and the new year celebrations begin with 'Sri Laitha sahasranama' homa. Her Devine grace Gurumatha Amma will initiate the thousand namas of Sri Lalitha to one thousand aspirants and the poornaahuthi is performed by one thousand people.
