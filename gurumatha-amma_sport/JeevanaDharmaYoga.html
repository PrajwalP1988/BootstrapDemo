Jeevana Dharma Yoga

Jeevana Dharma Yoga is where Vishnu shakthi is dominant.

An individual acquires self knowledge as a result of penetrating insight increasing inner vision and improved efficiency in work.

Jeevana Dharma Yoga is a life changing experience under the subtitle guidance of Gurumatha Amma. During their association with Amma, aspirants devote their time to learn the "spritual alphabet".

This is living life itself, the bliss is the consequence of your being.





Parama Poojya Gurumatha Amma

Those who approach Amma with an open mind seeking an answer to their questions find it her presence.

Amma helps people to achieve completeness through meditation, and anyone who is spiritual aspirant can undergo this experience.

Amma, who bestows enlightenment on people so that they can attain spiritual bliss by realizing the true form within themselves, achieves this by her spiritual energies. Her completeness helps others to realize theirs.

Since realizing truth is possible only by seeking refuge in the truth, this method is time-tested and contains the import of the Vedas and Upanishads.





Meditation

Meditation is not something to be practiced for one day.

Nor is it tied to traditions or conventions.

It rarely needs and aid and even the mantra used in meditation fails to yield results if one is not acquainted with the conditions to achieve the exalted state.

Anyone who has realied the self knows that man is born to elevate himself through meditation.

Life is for meditation and a state of complete health is consequence of only meditation.

Yagna Vignana

Religion has been reduced into a set of rituals which are observed either because of tradition or in expectation of some result.

This way, religion is slowly losing its importance in modern tomes. Homa and Havana (fire sacrifice) discovered by the sages, when performed with the right Vedic mantras, not only draw the cosmic energy towards the earth but enhance the positive vibrations of the five elements (earth, water, fire, air and sky). These vibrations, in turn, have positive results on our five-layered body, apart from helping us in controlling our five senses and achieving perfect mental balance. They elevate a person from mundane existence and liberate him from the three qualities that bind us to rebirth.

They also bestow spiritual as well as supernatural powers on Sadhaka and give him the qualities necessary to experience liberation from self. They help him go beyond the senses and give an insight into the six priced Vidhyas. Participate in such Homa whole heartedly and partake the spiritual bliss.

Rahoyaga

We are presenting a new science of Raho yagakrama, an alchemy of Vedic spirituality, to bring about spiritual revolution and evolution in each and every soul. Karma yogis offer sacrifice to the gods, Gnana yogis surrender their mental, spiritual and physical activity into the supreme fire.

Through yogic practices like ashtanga yoga, particularly specific mudras, heat is generated and spreads to the crown chakra, thereby melting the frozen nectar, Which then permeates through all the nadis (channels of energy) in the body.

This is the rahastarpana, the offering of Rahoyoga.

Performing inner yagas (sacrifice) is an important and necesary process to glimpse the divinity within ourselves and in the entire world.

As we introspect and touch the truth of our nature buried within ourseles, we become more refined, our energy increases and becomes more subtle. As a result, the desire to make the transition from mediocrity to greatness surfaces within each one of us.
