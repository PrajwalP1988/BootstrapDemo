Our Offerings

Process Monitoring

  • Our executives will interact with your representatives on daily basis for Reports/ MIS.
  • Review of MIS reports on a fortnightly and generation of a comprehensive report. In case of any change for further streamlining the operation, Transport Manager will discuss matter with Administration Dept.
  • In case of any deviation, the same may be escalated to Operation Executive and Operations Manager.

Scope of Contract / Basic Offering

  • Transport of Executives as per customer requirement.
  • Shift to Single Window Solution Provider in a phased manner, the time frame and priority to be decided by the client.
  • Any numbers of vehicles as per Customer Requirements.
  • Preventive maintenance of vehicles for minimizing the down time.
  • Stand by vehicles to be provided in case of any vehicle break – down.
  • MIS in relation to all transport operations would be provided by us for all the routes.

Value Additions

  • Standard Delivery Time – to be mutually agreed upon.
  • Service back-up – through networking of service stations.
  • Routing, Scheduling and Forecasting – by use of statistical optimization.
  • S.O.S in terms of break down management.
  • Transparency in monthly billing.


Employee Transportation

  • Entire Spectrum of Vehicles for Executive movement.
  • Routing and Scheduling.
  • Administrative and Back- up services.
  • Schedule Adherence.
  • Timely Pick-up and Drop.
  • SOS Help.

Vehicles

  • We provide vehicles to all needs - from an individual taste to a corporate need. Immaculately conditioned, new vehicles with seating capacity ranging from 4 + 1 to 48 + 2 seats shall be on the offering. Wherein, each vehicle is thoroughly checked by the Supervisors and handed over to the line supervisor who checks the acoustics, electronic devices and cleanliness of the upholstery. After a thorough checkup, the vehicle is certified fit for use of the clients. Thus a thorough servicing of each vehicle ensures a trouble free travel.
  • Personality development training programmes are frequently organized to enhance the work efficiency of our staff; be it Drivers, Supervisors or Managers

Operation

  • By operating 24 hours a day and 365 days in a year we ensure no demands go unattended, unsatisfied. And moving with the technology we’re equipped with a control unit –a help line which monitors and responds to call, immediately.
