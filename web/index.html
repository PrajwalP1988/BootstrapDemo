Yoganand Chandraiah

Animal Cognition Researcher and Wildlife Conservationist from INDIA.
I am a Commission Member of "World Commission on Environmental Law" - IUCN (International Union for Conservation of Nature).
Animals are my passion and exploring their behavior, cognition and ecological adaptations are my primary area of interest.
I love to educate kids, youngsters and people who are curious about animals and i too create awareness on Illegal Wildlife Trade and other serious environmental threats.
